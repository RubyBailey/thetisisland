## About TIRRA

The Thetis Island Residents and Ratepayers Association (TIRRA) is a volunteer-run organization that advocates for residents, coordinates essential services and fosters a strong island community. From managing solid waste and trails to supporting local initiatives, TIRRA plays a vital role in daily life on Thetis.
